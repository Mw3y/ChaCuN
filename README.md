    - What is ChaCuN?

>   ChaCuN, which stands for 'Chasseurs et Cueilleurs au NÃ©olithique' is an 
|   electronic version of the board game 'Chasseurs et Cueilleurs', derived 
|   from the famous 'Carcassonne'. It is the project of first year students 
|   enrolled in the summer 2024 EPFL Computer Science bachelor semester.

>   ChaCuN is designed to be played by 2 to 5 players, each aiming to build 
|   a prehistoric landscape by placing square tiles side by side. 
|   The different parts of the landscape, for example, forests, rivers, etc
|   can be occupied by hunters, gatherers or fishermen, to earn points.
